About the Resort

More than a resort.
It's an experience.

Since opening our doors in December 2010, Super Cruise Tours and Resort has been welcoming families, couples and travellers from around the world to our slice of Jamaican paradise. Over fifteen years on, we're still the same resort — just better.

Three levels of all-inclusive stays, eight restaurants, world-class activities and a team that genuinely loves what they do. Whatever brought you here, we'll make sure it's worth it.
